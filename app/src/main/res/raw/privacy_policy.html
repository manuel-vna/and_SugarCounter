1. Introduction

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the SugarCounter mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the app") collects, stores, and protects your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws. By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Personal Data Collected

Our app only stores personal data that you voluntarily input. The personal data stored includes:
  • Entries about your sugar consumption
  • Designations for the foodstuffs you have entered
  • Date of the sugar entry
We do not collect or store any other personal information. For example we do not store your name, email, post address, phone number, or age.

3. No Login or Authentication Required

Our app does not require a user account, login, or authentication. We do not have access to your data, nor do we transmit it to any external sources.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

Storage Location:
All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Data Retention:
The data remains stored as long as you choose to keep it. Since the data is stored on your device, you can delete it at any time of your choice.

Manual Deletion:
You can delete your data at any time manually within the app or with the help of the operating system.

Automatic Deletion:
If enabled, the app can automatically delete data older than a specific time frame.

Uninstalling the App:
When you uninstall the app, all stored data will be permanently deleted.

5. Tracking and Third-Party Data Transmission

Our app does not track your activity. No data is transmitted to any third-party services or servers. The only data that may be collected is crash reports and app performance data by the Google Play Console, which helps improve the app’s stability. We do not use cookies or analytics tools that track user behavior.

6. Security

We use appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, but we cannot guarantee absolute security. You should take measures to protect your device, such as using a secure lock screen and keeping your software up to date.

7. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Any changes will be reflected in the app. A user might review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect their data.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, you have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:
  • The right of information.
  • The right to access and download your stored data. This can be done within the app via 'Export entries'.
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data. Since we do not store any other data than your input data the deletion of the data is associated with deleting the app.

9. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us by email at jumparoundcreations@gmail.com.
